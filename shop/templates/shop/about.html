DK JOya (also Joiya, Joyia or Joria) belongs to Uttar Pardesh Baloch tribe of India.They are descended from Baloch tribesmen who settled in this region of North India in the late Middle Ages.James Tod places it in the list of Thirty Six Royal Races.The sub-clans of Joiya are Jatoiya Baloch,Uttar Pradesh Baluch, Rao, Rana and Jats etc.As this clan extended the Jats,Rao ,Rana and Rajputs and other clans of India joined it due to they were impressed by their bravery.All of above narrated are Johiyas.Joiya is derivated from "Yahya"means Muslim.Johiya were famous from their bravery and it was a warrior tribe that ruled in Northern Areas of India.After being defeated by rebellious Rajput of their own clan and Bikka , they were compelled to migrate due to the cruelty of Rajputs.Some of Johiya warriors joined Rajput forces for the sake of their life.The word "Joyia" is also derived from Ayudhiya because ancients of Johiya were inhibitant of Ayudhiya, India and then moved to Uttar Pradesh.Johiya are the descendant of warriors of Uttar Pradesh Baloch community.They are the great immigrants.They migrated from India to Pakistan and are still settled in Muzaffargarh,Kacchi Plain, Central Punjab and various areas of Pakistan.During migration they joined and stayed in Jatoi Baloch tribe in Kacchi Plain.They carried their race forward as Jatoi/Jatoiya Baloch.Johiya was not a caste it was the tribe of ancient Ayudhiya Warriors.Sheer M Johiya was the leader of this tribe who moved to Uttar Pradesh.The Johiya which are settled in Pakistan is the progeny of Uttar Pradesh Baloch tribe after migration from India to Pakistan.
